Weather
Here are some questions to inspire you:

    
	
  • Barometers are traditionally used to predict, wet or changeable weather ahead by measuring air pressure. How reliable is this method?
    • 
	
  • How do temperatures vary throughout the ear? How do daylight hours vary throughout the year? Are temperature and daylight related?
    • 
	
  • How does wind direction and speed affect temperature? How is wind speed related to air pressure? How is wind direction related to 
temperature?
    • 
	
  • How does rainfll throughout the year? What are the good predictors for rain? How does humidity vary throughout the year? Is humidity 
a good predictor for rain? 
    • 
	
  • Are any of these good predictors of storms? Are any of these good predictors for "good weather"?
